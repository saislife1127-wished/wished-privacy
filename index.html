Privacy Policy

Wished — AI Wishes & Cards

Effective: April 2026

Wished ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered greeting and wishing app built for Indian occasions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Wished app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Contact information — names, phone numbers, birthdays, and occasion dates that you manually add or import from your phone contacts. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Wish preferences — tone, relationship, and language settings you choose when generating wishes. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Generated content — wishes and AI-generated cards you create. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage data — anonymous usage information such as feature usage and crash reports, collected to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalised AI wishes and greeting cards via our secure server-side API
  • To schedule birthday and occasion reminders on your device
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To process in-app purchases for card credits

3. AI Processing

When you generate a wish, your selected occasion, tone, and any personal details you provide are sent to our secure Cloudflare Worker proxy, which calls OpenAI's API to generate the wish. We do not store your wish requests on our servers. OpenAI's data handling is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

When you generate an AI card, the occasion type and style are sent to fal.ai's image generation API. No personal information is included in image generation requests. fal.ai's data handling is governed by fal.ai's Privacy Policy.

4. Contact Data

All contact data you add in Wished is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not upload, sync, or store your contacts on any server. If you grant contacts permission, we read your phone contacts only to help you import them — we do not transmit your phone contacts to any server.

5. Advertising

Wished may show rewarded ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

6. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. Purchase records are handled by Google Play's billing system.

7. Data Retention

All data stored by Wished lives on your device. You can delete all app data at any time through your device's app settings. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

Wished is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
